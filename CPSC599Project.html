CPSC 599: Final Project

RGB Roman Wearable

Summary:

The final project of CSPC 599, Physical and Tangible HCI, focues on developing our understanding of hygge, and creating a TUI that is wearable, usable, and meaningful to its users. The main goal is to create something that can be used to calm a user, make them feel hygge, and provide a nice reminder of better times. For my project, I developed a wearable led shirt, called the RGB Roman, which has ten options for different led light shows, to allow for users to choose exactly what they want to show for a given moment. This can be used in parties, clubs, just alone time, and other festivities to assist in creating a sense of hygge for the user.
